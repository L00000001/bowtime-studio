Café Paradiso
VinylPrint		 Mykki Blanco / Transgressive Art Direction 2021 +
Client
Mykki Blanco
Format
Vinyl LP 12"
Discipline
Art Direction
Year
2021

The concept emerged from a napkin — literally. A real diner napkin, sourced from a specific café, photographed under cellophane. The sleeve becomes its own found object: printed on uncoated stock, the image sits slightly behind the surface. Nothing is designed that didn't already exist.

Art direction, photography, print specification. Offset lithography on 280gsm uncoated. Vinyl: black 180g.

GBIUS Inaugural Pin
ObjectEdition		 GBIUS Object Design 2022 +
Client
GBIUS
Format
Hard Enamel 38mm
Discipline
Object / Industrial
Edition
250

Form derives from an axial sonograph — a cross-section of sound mapped as landscape. The Pearl River Delta as seen from orbital altitude, compressed into a 38mm hard enamel object. Six colour fills, gold plating on the metal base.

Concept, form development, production liaison. Manufactured: Pearl River Delta. Edition of 250.

Bloobouba / Kxiikxxikiii
FragranceIdentity		 Luk Rix Product / Identity 2024 +
Brand
Luk Rix
Format
EDT 50ml
Discipline
Product / Copy
Status
Pre-Order

Two EDT fragrances as the inaugural Luk Rix scent objects. Names are phonetic — spelled to be sounded rather than read. Each ships with a multilingual notes booklet across nine languages. The booklet is the product as much as the bottle.

Creative direction, naming, packaging concept, multilingual copywriting. Bottled in France.
